Smoothie

A delicious and refreshing berry smoothie!

Perfect for a hot summer's day, this berry smoothie is sure to blow you away!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 cup frozen berries of choice
  • 1 tbsp chia
  • 1/4 cup milk (or alternative)

Steps

  1. Put everything into a blender
  2. Blend.
